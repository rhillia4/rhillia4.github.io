Website Evaluations

Good Site

Contrast

The contrast is good. The selection of colors is appealing to users.

Repitition

The repitition of styles is comforting as it makes it easy for the user to navigate the page.

Alignment

The use of space makes the page feel open but not empty. It also put emphasis on what is important.

Proximity

The elements are evenly spaced and close to likewise elements making the users experience easier.

Bad Site

Contrast

The contrast is good because the text is easily readable, but the selection of colors is not appealing to users.

Repitition

The page lacks repitition of styles and is discomforting each bad decision adds to the previous.

Alignment

The space is not fully utilized. What is used though, cramps the text tightly together giving an unwanted reading experience.

Proximity

There are elements on top of each other and inconsistent spacing.
