Meet Our Team

Our book club is led by a team of passionate individuals who love reading just as much as you do! From experienced book club veterans to those just starting their literary journey, our leadership team ensures that every meeting is engaging, inclusive, and enjoyable.

  • Julie Wall-Burris (President): A lover of historical fiction, Julie is committed to bringing fresh perspectives to every book selection and discussion.
  • Shannon (Vice President): Shannon is known for her insightful analysis of character development and her passion for mystery novels.
  • Margaret (Event Coordinator): Margaret keeps our calendar full of fun activities like author interviews, book swaps, and themed events.
