Project Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a comprehensive platform for managing the Madison Park Book Club. The site is designed to keep members and organizers informed, up to date on upcoming meetings, aware of the books being read, and prepared to lead or participate in discussions each month. It also offers an easy way to sign up for snacks and other contributions for the meetings.

Users of the Site

This website caters to two primary groups of users:

  • Organizers: Those who plan the meetings, manage schedules, and lead discussions.
  • Members: Those who participate in the discussions, sign up for snacks, and attend events.

Website Content Overview

The website contains the following key sections:

  • Book Club Information: A description of the book club, its mission, and how to join.
  • Upcoming Schedule: An event page of upcoming meetings, group leaders, locations and dates.
  • Book Information: Details on the current book, including a brief summary, author bio, and discussion lead.
  • Members Page: Details on the current members, including a brief summary and their role.
  • Sign Up Page: Details on the current book, including a brief summary, author bio, and discussion lead.
