How to Play Golf & Improve Your Game

Basic Golf Techniques

  1. The Grip – Holding the club correctly is key to making consistent shots.
  2. The Stance – Positioning your feet and body for a balanced swing.
  3. The Swing – A smooth motion from backswing to follow-through.

Common Golf Shots

  • Drive – A long-distance shot usually taken from the tee.
  • Chip – A short shot to get the ball onto the green.
  • Putt – A gentle stroke used to roll the ball into the hole.

Improving Your Game

  • Take lessons or watch instructional videos.
  • Practice at the driving range and putting green.
  • Play regularly and track your progress.
