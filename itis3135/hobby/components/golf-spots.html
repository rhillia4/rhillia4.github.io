Best Places to Play Golf

Golf courses vary widely in design and difficulty, offering different playing experiences.

Famous Golf Courses

  • Augusta National (USA) – Home of the Masters Tournament.
  • St. Andrews (Scotland) – Known as the "Home of Golf," one of the oldest courses.
  • Pebble Beach (USA) – Stunning ocean views and challenging holes.

Types of Golf Courses

  1. Links Courses – Built along the coast, featuring natural dunes and windy conditions.
  2. Parkland Courses – More traditional, with trees and well-manicured fairways.
  3. Desert Courses – Found in arid regions, with sandy terrain and minimal grass.

Local & Public Courses

Many cities and towns have public courses, which provide affordable options for golfers of all levels. Golf simulators and driving ranges are also great for practicing year-round.
