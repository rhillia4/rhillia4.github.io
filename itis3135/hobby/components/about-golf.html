About Golf

Golf is a sport that dates back centuries, originating in Scotland. It involves using clubs to hit a small ball into a series of holes in as few strokes as possible. The game is played on courses featuring different terrains, hazards, and strategic challenges.

About Me & My Interest in Golf

I first became interested in golf because of its mix of skill, patience, and strategy. It’s a game that requires focus and precision, and it’s also a great way to relax outdoors. Whether I’m playing a full 18-hole round or just practicing at the driving range, golf always challenges me to improve.
