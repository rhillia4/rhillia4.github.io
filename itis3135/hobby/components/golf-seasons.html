Best Times to Play Golf

Golf can be played year-round in some locations, but the best time depends on the climate and conditions.

Seasonal Considerations

  • Spring & Fall – Ideal temperatures and mild conditions make these the best times to play.
  • Summer – Longer days allow for more play, but high temperatures can make it exhausting.
  • Winter – In warmer regions, winter golf is possible, but in colder areas, courses may close.

Time of Day

  • Morning (6 AM - 10 AM) – Cooler temperatures, less crowded.
  • Afternoon (10 AM - 3 PM) – Warmer and windier, with more players on the course.
  • Evening (3 PM - Sunset) – Relaxed pace, beautiful views, but limited daylight.

Major Golf Tournaments

  • The Masters (April) – One of the most prestigious golf tournaments.
  • U.S. Open (June) – Known for challenging course conditions.
  • The Open Championship (British Open) (July) – The oldest golf tournament.
